ABOUT

Vishnu Engineering Works

Vishnu Engineering Works, based in Ahmedabad, is a trusted workshop led by Mr. Rajeshkumar, who brings over 30 years of hands-on experience in die and model manufacturing. Since the early 1990s, we have been serving industries with precision, durability, and consistent quality

We specialize in custom machine die making, plastic injection mold designing, and metal die fabrication — using advanced machinery including CNC and VMC setups to ensure accuracy and efficiency in every project.

Over the years, our workshop has built a reputation for delivering quality tools on time, whether it’s a small precision part or a full-scale industrial die. We believe in engineering excellence, client trust, and long-term relationships.

Our mission is to provide cost-effective, long-lasting die solutions that support the growth of Indian manufacturers and industries.

SERVICES

 Custom Machine Die Making

Custom-made machine dies as per your exact need. Made using strong material and accurate design. Reliable for all types of industrial use.

Plastic Injection Mold Design

We design durable plastic molds with high precision. Ideal for industries like packaging, pharma, and electricals. Clean output and long-lasting performance.

Metal Die Model Fabrication

We build CNC-machined metal dies with tight precision. Best for cutting, stamping, and shaping jobs. Long life and industrial-grade strength.

Die Repair & Modification

Repair and modify damaged dies quickly and accurately. Cost-effective solution to extend die life. Improved fit, finish, and function.
